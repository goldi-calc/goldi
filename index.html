SUSPENSION & PUNISHMENT POLICY

Goldi — Punishment Policy

Last updated: June 2026
best1111eren_founderreynddd
⚖️
This policy governs all disciplinary actions taken against players of Goldi. Punishments are enforced by the moderation team and are final unless appealed through the official appeal process. All decisions are logged and reviewed before execution.
PUNISHMENT TIERS
TIERNAMEDESCRIPTION
T1WarningFormal written warning. No collection impact.
T2Partial RemovalIllegitimately gained or relevant assets removed + 1–3 day suspension.
T3Partial Removal + SuspensionBroader removal (Pokémon, cosmetics, currency) + 7–14 day suspension.
T4Full Removal + SuspensionEntire collection wiped + 30–60 day suspension.
T5Permanent SuspensionAccount permanently banned. Full collection wiped. No appeal after second offence.

⚠ Collection removal is NEVER issued alone — it always comes with at least a short suspension.

VIOLATIONS & PUNISHMENTS
01Exploitation & Glitch Abuse▼

Intentionally using bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics to gain items, currency, Pokémon, or cosmetics beyond normal gameplay.

SEVERITYPUNISHMENT
First offence — minor exploit, small gainT2 Illegitimate gains removed + 1-day suspension
Second offence — repeated or moderate gainT3 Broader removal + 7-day suspension
Third offence — large scale or deliberateT4 Full Collection Removal + 30-day suspension
Severe / coordinated exploit abuseT5 Full Collection Removal + Permanent Suspension
02Cheating & Unauthorized Tools▼

Using self-bots, macro tools, scripts, or any third-party automation to interact with Goldi commands.

SEVERITYPUNISHMENT
First offenceT3 Partial Collection Removal + 14-day suspension
Second offenceT4 Full Collection Removal + 60-day suspension
Third offenceT5 Full Collection Removal + Permanent Suspension
03Account Sharing & Boosting▼

Sharing an account with another player, or using a second account to transfer items, currency, or cosmetics to a main account.

SEVERITYPUNISHMENT
First offence — minor sharingT2 Transferred assets removed + 1-day suspension
Second offence — deliberate boostingT3 Partial removal on both accounts + 7-day suspension
Third offence or alt account abuseT4 Full removal on all linked accounts + 30-day suspension
Large scale multi-account farmingT5 Full Collection Removal + Permanent Suspension (all accounts)
04Currency & Item Duplication▼

Exploiting any method to duplicate ARCs, items, pokeballs, cosmetics, or any other in-game asset.

SEVERITYPUNISHMENT
First offence — small duplicationT2 Duplicated assets removed + 3-day suspension
Second offenceT3 Partial Collection Removal + 14-day suspension
Third offence or mass duplicationT4 Full Collection Removal + 60-day suspension
Deliberate mass duplication / distributionT5 Full Collection Removal + Permanent Suspension
05Harassment & Toxic Behaviour▼

Using Goldi commands, trade features, or any bot interaction to target, harass, intimidate, or demean another player.

SEVERITYPUNISHMENT
First offenceT1 Warning
Second offence3-day suspension
Third offence14-day suspension
Severe or targeted harassment30-day to Permanent Suspension depending on impact
06Staff Impersonation & Bypass Abuse▼

Pretending to be a staff member, abusing staff permissions, or attempting to manipulate staff decisions. Includes misuse of bypass commands.

SEVERITYPUNISHMENT
First offenceT3 Partial Collection Removal + 14-day suspension
Second offenceT5 Full Collection Removal + Permanent Suspension
Bypass command misuse (non-staff)T5 Full Collection Removal + Permanent Suspension
07Real Money Trading (RMT)▼

Selling, buying, or trading in-game assets, accounts, or progress for real money or external goods/services. Both buyer and seller are punished equally.

SEVERITYPUNISHMENT
First offenceT4 Full Collection Removal + 60-day suspension
Second offence or confirmed transactionT5 Full Collection Removal + Permanent Suspension
08False Reporting & Malicious Appeals▼

Submitting false reports against other players or fabricating evidence in an appeal to gain an unfair outcome.

SEVERITYPUNISHMENT
First offenceT1 Warning
Second offence7-day suspension
Fabricated evidence submitted30-day suspension + appeal rights revoked

APPEALS

  • Players may appeal T3, T4, and T5 punishments only.
  • Appeals must be submitted within 7 days of punishment.
  • Appeals are reviewed by a senior moderator not involved in the original case.
  • Permanent suspensions after a second T5 offence are NOT eligible for appeal.
  • Submitting a false appeal may result in additional punishment (Section 08).
