Data & Privacy

Effective Date: 12 February 2025

This privacy policy explains what data Goldi collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

The Bot may collect and store the following data:

  • User IDs and Server IDs (to track preferences or settings)
  • Messages or commands sent to the Bot (for functionality)
  • Tournament data or game statistics (if applicable)

We do not collect:

  • Direct messages between users
  • Any sensitive personal information

2. How We Use Data

Data is used solely to provide features of the Bot, such as tracking tournament progress, displaying badge and profile information, managing auctions, quiz events, or server-specific configurations. It is not sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to secure data but cannot guarantee absolute security. Stored data is kept only as long as necessary to maintain Bot functionality.

4. User Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting the bot owner via the official Discord server: discord.gg/F7JJG8cw8w

5. Changes to the Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes implies acceptance.
